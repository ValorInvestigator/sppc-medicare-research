## Key Facts at a Glance

- **Program name:** State Plan Personal Care (SPPC) / PC20
- **Federal authority:** SSA Section 1905(a)(24); 42 CFR 440.167
- **Oregon rules:** OAR 411-034 (APD track), OAR 411-455 (ODDS/IDD track)
- **Entitlement:** YES — no waitlist, no enrollment cap
- **Nursing facility LOC required:** NO (unlike K Plan, APD Waiver, ICP)
- **Max hours:** 270/year (APD track); minimum 9.18 hrs per service period
- **Income limit:** $967/month (single), $1,450/month (couple)
- **Asset limit:** $2,000 (single), $3,000 (couple)
- **Spousal pay:** NOT allowed under SPPC
- **CCO involvement:** NONE — LTSS carved out of CCO budgets
- **Administering agency:** ODHS/APD (elderly/disabled) or ODHS/ODDS (IDD)
- **Medicare covers personal care:** NO — SPPC fills this gap for dual-eligibles
- **Agency rate:** $39.40/hr (Jan 1, 2026)
- **HCW wage:** $21.25/hr (Jan 1, 2026, SEIU CBA)
- **EVV system:** eXPRS
- **Billing contact:** dmap.personalcare@state.or.us

## Oregon LTSS Program Ladder

```
Income too high for Medicaid → OPI-M (400% FPL, no MERP, no NFLOC req.)
        ↓
Qualifies for OSIPM/ABD Medicaid, needs ADL help, below NFLOC → SPPC
        ↓
Needs escalate to Nursing Facility Level of Care → K Plan (1915k CFC)
        ↓
Needs community transition or housing support (waitlist) → APD 1915(c) Waiver
        ↓
Can no longer be safely maintained at home → Nursing Facility
        ↓
Age 55+, NFLOC, in PACE service area → PACE (all-inclusive)
```
